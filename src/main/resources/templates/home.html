About Poovar Island Resort

Poovar Island Resort is a beautiful and tranquil destination located in the southern state of Kerala, India. It offers a unique experience for travelers seeking a peaceful and rejuvenating getaway. The resort is located on a remote, isolated island off the coast of Sri Lanka, providing guests with a secluded and serene environment to unwind and reconnect with nature.

Poovar Island Resort is nestled amidst the natural beauty of the Poovar Estuary, where the Neyyar River meets the Arabian Sea. This pristine location provides breathtaking views and a serene atmosphere, making it an ideal escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

The resort offers a variety of accommodation options, including deluxe rooms, premium suites, and family suites, all designed to provide comfort and luxury to guests. Guests can explore the natural beauty of Poovar, including beautiful landscapes, lush greenery, and serene backwaters, allowing them to immerse themselves in the surrounding natural environment.

Known for its world-class hospitality, Poovar Island Resort's staff is friendly and attentive, ensuring that guests have a memorable and enjoyable stay. The resort offers a range of activities, such as boat rides on the backwaters, nature walks, and Ayurvedic spa treatments, allowing guests to relax and rejuvenate in the lap of nature.
